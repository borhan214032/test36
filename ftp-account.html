How to Create And Setup an FTP Account in cPanel

To create an FTP account you'll need to log into cPanel. If you do not have your cPanel information you'll need to access your Welcome email we sent when you completed your order at PeoplesHost. This Welcome email will contain your cPanel login information; cPanel URL, cPanel Username, and cPanel Password. You will need this information to continue to follow this tutorial.

Now that you have your cPanel login information on hand, you can follow these instructions to add an FTP account through your cPanel.


Step 1: Login to cPanel

Step 2: Click the FTP Accounts icon under the Files section

Step 3: Add FTP Account

Click the Create FTP Account button

Now you have successfully added a new FTP Account in cPanel!
